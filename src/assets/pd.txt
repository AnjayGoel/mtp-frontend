   For example, lets take a look at the famous prisoners dilemma.

        Imagine that the police arrested you and your friend for a crime. Both of you are held in different cells and
        cannot communicate with each other. The police officer offers you both the opportunity to either remain
        silent or blame another suspect. If both of you suspects remain silent, both will serve only one year in prison.
        If you both blame each other, both will serve five years in prison.
